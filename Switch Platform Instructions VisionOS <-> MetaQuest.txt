Instructions to switch platform between Meta Quest 3 and Apple Vision Pro:

To switch to Vision OS:
Step 0: Close the Unity project
Step 1: Delete the Packages/manifest.json file
Step 2: Duplicate the Packages/manifest_visionos.json file, and rename the new file to "manifest.json"
Step 3: Re-open the Unity project

To switch to Meta Quest:
Step 0: Close the Unity project
Step 1: Delete the Packages/manifest.json file
Step 2: Duplicate the Packages/manifest_metaquest.json file, and rename the new file to "manifest.json"
Step 3: Re-open the Unity project
